About me

About:

Welcome to my creative space! I'm Rishikesh Sharad Memane, a passionate Data geek based in Pune ,Maharashtra, India. With a knack for Data Analytics, I embark on a journey to transform raw data into captivating insights.

Background:

Armed with a degree in Bachelors Degree in Printing Engineering from University of Pune, my journey in the realm of data began in late 2021. From the foundations of statistical analysis to the intricacies of machine learning algorithms, I've cultivated a skill set that revolves around understanding and harnessing the power of data.

Skills:

Being a Sales Intern and later a Data Scientist Intern, I’ve developed and tweaked my data analyst and statistical analysis skills. Over the last year, I have worked on developing SQL, Power BI, Machine Learning, Python and Excel skills through various online and offline platforms. .

Beyond Data:

When I'm not knee-deep in datasets, you can find me on the football field, scoring goals and enjoying the beautiful game. I'm also deeply passionate about design, where I explore the intersection of creativity and functionality. Additionally, I have a love for traveling, discovering new cultures, and embracing diverse perspectives. These interests—football, design, and traveling—provide a well-rounded perspective that enhances my problem-solving skills. Whether it's a strategic play, a visually compelling design, or an enriching travel experience, I bring the same level of enthusiasm and dedication to everything I do.
